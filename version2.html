📖 About Readathon 2026

Readathon 2026 is an initiative to inspire students to develop a lifelong reading habit. Students submit their daily reading using a Google Form. The website automatically displays live statistics, reader rankings and achievements. This project also supports educational research on how regular reading improves learning, vocabulary, confidence and critical thinking.
